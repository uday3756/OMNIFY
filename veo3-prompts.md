# Veo3 Prompts for the Omnify AI Demo

Paste these into Veo3 to generate the brand video. Keep the look consistent:
**mint and cream tones, soft natural light, friendly, cinematic, shallow depth of field.**
Aspect ratio 16:9 for the website hero and landing demo, 9:16 for the social story.

---

## A. Hero ambient clip (loops behind the discovery headline)
> A bright, airy scene of kids doing different activities (robotics, painting, swimming, dance) in quick gentle cuts, soft mint and cream color grade, warm natural light, slow dreamy camera moves, shallow depth of field, no text, seamless loop feel, 8 seconds.

Drop the result in `video/hero.mp4` (the file the scroll-scrubbed hero plays).

---

## B. 30-second product demo (frame by frame)

**Frame 1 (0-5s).** Warm, soft-lit living room. A 30s mother on a couch scrolling a phone, slightly overwhelmed. Mint and cream tones, shallow depth of field, gentle handheld motion, cinematic, 5s.

**Frame 2 (5-12s).** Extreme close-up of a clean mobile text field on mint, a green pulsing mic icon, dictated words appearing: "7 year old loves robots, free weekends." Crisp UI, soft glow, smooth macro motion, 6s.

**Frame 3 (12-18s).** Motion graphic: a handwritten sentence dissolves into floating rounded tag chips (age 7, robots, shy, weekends, sibling, trial) lighting up green in sequence. White space, soft particles, satisfying spring motion, 6s.

**Frame 4 (18-25s).** Three clean program cards slide up on a mint background, each with a kid-activity photo and a green match badge (96, 91, 88). A finger taps the first. Bright, friendly UI animation, soft shadow, 6s.

**Frame 5 (25-30s).** A happy 7-year-old boy building a small robot at a bright classroom table, laughing with another child. Warm natural light. Smash cut to a green checkmark and the omnify logo on a clean mint card. Joyful, cinematic, 5s.

---

## C. Social teaser (9:16, 6 seconds)
> Vertical video. Fast, playful montage: a parent says a sentence, it turns into glowing tags, three class cards appear, a kid laughs at a robotics table. Mint and cream brand colors, upbeat, snappy cuts, on-screen text "Tell us about your kid. We find their thing." 6 seconds.

---

### Tips
- Keep "omnify" lowercase and pair the green dot.
- If Veo3 adds text you did not ask for, add "no text" to the prompt.
- Generate Frame 3 and Frame 4 as UI motion, then composite the real screen recordings on top for crispness.
